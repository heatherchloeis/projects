about Indigeny Reserve

Come Visit Us Today. Drive along a secluded tree-lined road, over the covered bridge, and leave the world behind. Our little corner of Sonora possesses the perfect climate and micro-climate for superior apple growing. We make hand-crafted hard cider and apple brandy in oak barrels. Our crisp and refreshing hard cider is gluten-free. And our apple brandy is organic and sustainably-produced. We keep a number of our hard cider varieties on tap as well as our line of infused vodkas and our specialty apply brandy for tastings. And those interested may also request a private tour of the facility including a peek of our cedar wood barrel room where the real magic happens.

the Watsons

Co-founders Jay and Judy Watson opened Indigeny Reserve to the public back in 2012, and already the distillery has become a celebrated travel destination. Both Jay and Judy left their previous careers to pursue a life focused on creating and encouraging a sustainability-driven lifestyle. They pride themselves on their involvement in the local music scene and often provide opportunities for young musicians to perform at the reserve.
